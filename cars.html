Mercedes W12 (LEGO):

The W12 was Mercades' entrant into the 2021 F1 Season. It won 9 of the 22 races it entered, winning the Constructor's Championship. The W12 and the 2021 season saw Lewis Hamilton controversially lose what would have been his eighth driver's title in a nail biting fight against Max Verstappen

This particular LEGO car is part of the Speed Champions collection. The car was a decently fun and quick build, obviously symmetrical. The most challenging part was honestly attaching the Petronas stickers that Mercedes-AMG were made to endure. The set comes with wet weather tyres and both the yellow and black camera pod so you can decide if you'd like Bottas or Hamilton sitting on your shelf.

Car Summary:

  • Wins:9
  • Podiums:28
  • Poles:9
  • Engine: Mercedes V6 hybrid Formula One power unit
  • Drivers: Lewis Hamilton, Valtteri Bottas
